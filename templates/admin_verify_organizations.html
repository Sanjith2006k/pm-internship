Verification Checklist

Before approving an organization, verify all four requirements below:

CIN Verification

  • Check CIN number format
  • Visit MCA.gov.in
  • Search CIN in database
  • Verify company name matches

LinkedIn Check

  • Visit company LinkedIn profile
  • Verify company exists & active
  • Check employee count (legitimate)
  • Confirm company website link

Domain Email

  • ✓ Must be company domain (e.g., @company.com)
  • ✗ Reject if Gmail/Yahoo
  • ✓ Matches company domain
  • Email format is valid

Certificate Verification

  • Download registration certificate PDF
  • Verify it matches CIN
  • Check certificate authenticity
  • Confirm company details match
