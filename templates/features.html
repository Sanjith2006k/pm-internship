Platform Features

PM Internship combines AI intelligence with powerful tools for students and organizations

Overview

PM Internship combines traditional internship management tools with advanced artificial intelligence to create a smarter and more efficient internship platform.

🤖

AI Internship Matching

The platform uses AI algorithms to analyze student profiles, skills, interests, and academic backgrounds to recommend the most suitable internships. This helps students find opportunities that align with their career goals.

📄

AI Resume Analysis

Students can upload their resumes and receive AI-powered feedback. The system evaluates resume structure, skills, and keywords and suggests improvements to increase their chances of being selected.

🎯

AI Candidate Shortlisting

Organizations can save time during recruitment with AI-powered candidate recommendations. The system analyzes student profiles and automatically highlights the most suitable applicants for each internship.

📊

AI Skill Gap Detection

The platform identifies missing skills that may affect a student's chances of getting selected for internships and recommends improvements or learning areas.

🔍

Smart Internship Search

Students can easily search internships using filters:

Skills Location Industry Duration Remote / On-site
🎓

Student Dashboard

Students receive a personalized dashboard to track internship applications, view recommended internships, update skills and profile information, and receive notifications.

🏢

Organization Dashboard

Companies can efficiently manage internship recruitment by posting opportunities, reviewing applications, shortlisting candidates, and tracking hiring progress.

✅

Verified Organization System

To ensure safe and genuine opportunities, organizations must go through a verification process before posting internships on the platform.

🔔

Real-Time Notification System

Students and organizations receive instant notifications for application updates, internship recommendations, interview invitations, and platform announcements.

🛡️

Admin Control Panel

The admin dashboard manages the entire platform by verifying organizations, monitoring internships, managing students and companies, and tracking platform statistics.
